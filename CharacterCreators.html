Concept Artwork for Dungeon Delvers (unfinished game)

A group of friends were in the process of creating a turn-based RPG video game, similiar to a tabletop RPGs such as Dungeons and Dragons. I was asked to help with the artwork, mainly the backgrounds and enemy concept art. While there was a lot of talk and brainstorming, our development was put on hold due to exams as well as the current pandemic.

Background Thumbnails

These thumbnails were developed off of very simple level titles, only giving me a central theme to work with. Some ideas were came very quickly while others, took some time and discussion with my friends.

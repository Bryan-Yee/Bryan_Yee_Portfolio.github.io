How The Internment Camps Affected Japanese Americans

In my BWRIT 135 course, we were tasked to write a paper on (REASON) and I chose to write mine on Japanese American Internment and how it affected them mentally. As a Yonsei, or fourth generation Japanese American, I wanted to learn more about the internment camps as it's a subject that's rarely brought up, both in my family and in my schooling, despite being such a big part of my Japanese American culture.

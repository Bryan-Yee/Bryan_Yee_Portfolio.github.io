ABOUT ME!

My name is Bryan Yee and I'm a sophomore at the University of Washington, Bothell campus. I'm a multimedia artist that has learned multiple coding languages, taught myself how to draw, 3D model and laser-cut wood. I have a fascination with storytelling and how one can convey stories through various mediums such as with audio, visuals, user input and interactive mediums that use all three such as video games or tabletop RPGs such as Dungeons and Dragons.

ABOUT ME!

My name is Bryan Yee and I'm a sophomore at the University of Washington, Bothell campus. I enjoy drawing, creating stories, making music, and playing video games. I'm a sort of a jack of all trades; I learned multiple coding languages such as HTML, Javascript, p5.js, processing, taught myself how to draw and 3D model, and have 8 years of experience playing the clarinet. I have a facination with storytelling and how you're able to convey storeis through various mediums such as with audio, visuals and quite recently, interactive mediums such as video games.
